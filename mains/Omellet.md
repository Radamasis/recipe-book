Ingredients
    2-3 large eggs
    1 tbsp butter
    1 pinch salt
    Optional: 1/4 cup  mozzarella cheese and fillings (e.g. spinach, onions, mushrooms) 

Instructions
    - Whisk the eggs until they're fluffy
    - Heat the skillet over medium-low heat and melt the butter
    - Pour the egg mixture into the pan. As the eggs begin to set, use a silicone spatula to pull the cooked edges 
    towards the center, tilting the pan so the uncooked eggs flow underneath.
    - When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add your fillings
    - Serve - plate the omellet and enjoy!
